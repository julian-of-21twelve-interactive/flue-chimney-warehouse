Therminox

THERMINOX is a high quality, multi-fuel, stainless steel twin wall insulated chimney system. It is designed for use with all solid fuel, wood, coal, gas, oil and pellet heating appliances.

Product Details

  • Exceptional thermal properties that guarantee a true 50 mm clearance to combustible materials at 1000°C
  • Solid Fire-Stop Plate at ceiling level in the room of the appliance.
  • The easy push-fit joint being fully insulated ensures no hot spots.
  • Every component is supplied with a recessed Locking Band and a safety ring that guarantees the security of the joint for life.
  • Quick and easy to install.
  • Wide range of accessories i.e. Terminals, adjustable lengths, elbows, tees, brackets & supports.
  • All of the products offered by Cheminées Poujoulat have been rigorously tested in every configuration by the CERIC Laboratory to ensure they meet the highest standards (Building Regulation Document J - Amendments to Approved Documents).

Efficience

20mm insulated triple wall system chimney for wood burning stoves
The Efficience chimney system is a concentric insulated chimney with mineral wool insulation allowing the connection of combustion air and the smoke exhaust. This system has been designed to operate safely with the latest generation of high efficiency wood burning stoves and is suitable for use in all types of air tight housing, new build or renovation projects.

This chimney system works in negative pressure and optimises the appliance’s efficiency and can be used on top or rear connection appliances.

Product Details

  • Optimise the performance of the appliance.
  • Avoid down-draught issues.
  • Pre-fabricated chimney stack available.
  • Available in any RAL colours.
  • All of the products offered by Cheminées Poujoulat have been rigorously tested in every configuration by the CERIC Laboratory to ensure they meet highest standards (Building Regulation Document J - Amendments to Approved Documents).
